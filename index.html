In the current NW Louisiana market, well-priced homes in Minden and Webster Parish typically receive offers within 2–6 weeks. Homes that are strategically priced and properly prepared tend to sell significantly faster. I can give you a realistic timeline based on your specific property and current comparable sales.

You don't have to — but it's strongly recommended. A pre-approval letter tells sellers you're a serious buyer, strengthens your offer, and helps you focus your search on homes in your real budget. I can connect you with trusted local lenders who know the NW Louisiana market.

In Louisiana, buyers typically pay 2–5% of the purchase price in closing costs, which includes lender fees, title insurance, prepaid taxes and insurance, and other items. Sellers typically cover the real estate agent compensation and their own share of closing costs. I walk every client through an estimated closing cost breakdown before we make or accept any offer — no surprises.
